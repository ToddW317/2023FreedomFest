Cadillac Freedom Festival Vendors

Be a Part of Freedom Festival 2024 as a Vendor!

We invite local shops, food vendors, and businesses to be a part of Freedom Festival 2024! This is your opportunity to showcase your products and services to thousands of festival-goers while being part of an event that celebrates our community and culture.

  • Vendor Opportunities:

    We're looking for a variety of vendors, including:

    • Artisans and Craft Merchants: Display and sell your unique, handcrafted goods.

    • Local Businesses: Promote your services and engage with the community.
  • How to Apply:

    Interested in being a vendor?
    Here’s how:

    • Fill out our online application form below.

    • Provide details about your products or services.

    • Submit any required fees.

    • Done! You are now registered!
  • Vendor Requirements:

    All vendors must comply with the below requirements:

    • Vendors are responsible for their own setup, including tents, tables, and chairs.

    • A nominal fee applies for vendor space, contributing towards festival maintenance and security.

    • Please see below for more information.

General Information:

  • Event Location: Cadillac Commons, approximately at 190 W. Cass Street, Cadillac, MI 49601


  • Event Dates:

  • 10am - 6pm:

  • Friday, July 5th, 2024
  • Saturday, July 6th, 2024


  • Application DEADLINE is 06/01/2024. There will be no refunds once accepted.

  • You will be responsible for your own tents, tables, and chairs. Please keep your area clean.

  • Booth setup is on Friday morning from 7am to 9:30am
    Security will be available on Friday night only.

  • Please remove your vehicle after unloading. Please park your vehicle away from the Event Area to allow the customer room to park.

  • Wi-Fi is not available in the park; please provide your own cellular service.

  • Use of generators is allowed but must be approved ahead of time and be kept away from the public in an approved location.

  • *The Cadillace Freedom Festival and the City of Cadillac are not responsible for any loss or damage to dispays, crafts, or for any injury, accident or theft occurring at the events.
