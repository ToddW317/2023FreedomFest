Kids & Pet Parade

Staging: 5:30 pm, Start: 6 pm

Commons - Behind After 26

Load up the kids and the pets for this mini parade down Lake Street. Costumes are encouraged!

  • Meet in the parking lot behind After 26
  • The parade will go down Chestnut St. to the Skate Park
  • Candy is allowed. Gently toss.
  • Prizes for the best pet costume and most creative pet/owner outfit
  • Kids can also be in the parade without a pet.
Fee is $10 or a donation of cat food, dog food, litter, treats, training pads, pet beds, etc. These items will be donated to the Wexford County Humane Society and Missaukee Humane Society.
